About me

My name is Diego Castillo Samperio, I am a currently a senior studying Computer Science at McKendree University.
I was born on April 18 in Guadalajara, Jalisco in Mexico. I have always played sports,
I started doing karate when I was 5 years old and got to be a black belt at the age of 10, however at 11 years old I decided to
leave karate and start my tennis career. When I started playing tennis, I knew it was going to be an important part in my life,
I continued playing tennis all the way trough high school, I was part of one of the best high school/college team in the country,
however I always wanted to play college tennis in the United States.

When I turned 17, I had to start looking for colleges in the United States, I had Division 1 offers, as well as Division 2 offers
to play tennis and get my undergraduate degree in Computer Science, ultimately I decided to commit to McKendree University,
where I received academic and athletic scholarship to play in the Men's Tennis Team.
